ITEM 1. BUSINESS

Orbis Financial Technologies Inc. is a financial technology company providing AI-powered payment processing solutions for small and medium-sized businesses across North America. The Company was incorporated in Nevada in 2019 and commenced operations in 2020.

Our proprietary platform, OrbisPay, processes over 2.3 million transactions monthly with an aggregate value exceeding $840 million. We serve approximately 14,500 active merchants across 38 states and 3 Canadian provinces.

ITEM 2. PROPERTIES
Our principal offices are located at 550 Montgomery Street, Suite 900, San Francisco, California, where we lease approximately 8,200 square feet under a lease expiring in December 2028.

ITEM 3. LEGAL PROCEEDINGS
We are party to a civil lawsuit filed in the Northern District of California by a former employee alleging wrongful termination. The case is in early discovery and we believe the claims are without merit. We do not expect this matter to have a material adverse effect on our financial condition.
