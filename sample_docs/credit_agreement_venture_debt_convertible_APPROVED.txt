VENTURE DEBT AND CONVERTIBLE LOAN AGREEMENT

This Venture Debt and Convertible Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of
March 14, 2026, between:

SILICON VALLEY GROWTH FINANCE LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and registered
investment advisor, with principal offices at 3000 Sand Hill Road, Suite 210, Menlo Park,
CA 94025 ("Lender"), and

QUANTUMLEAP TECHNOLOGIES, INC., a Delaware corporation with principal offices at
601 Townsend Street, Suite 500, San Francisco, CA 94103 (the "Borrower").

RECITALS

Borrower is a venture-backed Series B company developing quantum-accelerated machine learning
chips. Lender has agreed to extend venture debt financing to Borrower on the terms and
conditions set forth herein, alongside conversion rights as partial consideration.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE I — LOAN FACILITY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1.1  Facility Amount: USD 12,000,000 (twelve million US dollars), in two tranches:
     - Tranche A: USD 8,000,000 available at closing
     - Tranche B: USD 4,000,000 available upon achievement of ARR milestone of USD 5M
       (draw period: 6 months from Tranche A closing)

1.2  Maturity Date: 36 months from the Tranche A closing date.

1.3  Interest Rate: 9.5% per annum, payable monthly in arrears. Interest-only period:
12 months, after which equal monthly principal-plus-interest instalments apply.

1.4  Conversion Rights: Lender has the right (but not the obligation) to convert up to
USD 3,000,000 of outstanding principal into Borrower's equity securities at a 20%
discount to the price per share of the Borrower's next Qualified Financing Event (defined
as a financing raising at least USD 15,000,000).

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE II — REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

As of the date hereof and as of each drawdown date, Borrower represents and warrants:

2.1  Organisation and Good Standing: Borrower is duly incorporated, validly existing, and
in good standing under the laws of Delaware. Borrower is duly qualified to do business in
each jurisdiction where its operations require qualification.

2.2  Authority: The execution, delivery, and performance of this Agreement have been duly
authorised by all necessary corporate action. This Agreement constitutes the legal, valid,
and binding obligation of the Borrower, enforceable in accordance with its terms.

2.3  Financial Statements: The financial statements provided to Lender fairly present
the financial condition of the Borrower as of their respective dates, prepared in
accordance with U.S. GAAP on a consistent basis.

2.4  No Material Adverse Change: Since December 31, 2025, there has been no material
adverse change in the business, operations, properties, assets, or financial condition
of the Borrower.

2.5  Intellectual Property: Borrower owns or holds valid licences to all intellectual
property necessary to conduct its business. No claims have been asserted challenging
Borrower's ownership or right to use such intellectual property.

2.6  Litigation: No material litigation, arbitration, or governmental proceeding is
pending or, to the Borrower's knowledge, threatened against Borrower.

2.7  Compliance with Laws: Borrower is in compliance in all material respects with all
applicable laws, regulations, and governmental orders.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE III — EVENTS OF DEFAULT CLAUSE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

3.1  Events of Default: Each of the following shall constitute an "Event of Default":

     (a) Payment Default: Failure to pay any principal or interest when due, not cured
         within five (5) business days after written notice from Lender;

     (b) Covenant Breach: Material breach of any covenant, obligation, or undertaking
         under this Agreement not remedied within thirty (30) days of written notice;

     (c) Representation and Warranty Breach: Any representation or warranty proves
         materially false or misleading when made or deemed made;

     (d) Cross-Default: Default under any other indebtedness of Borrower exceeding
         USD 500,000 in aggregate, if such default gives the relevant creditor the right
         to accelerate;

     (e) Insolvency: (i) Borrower commences a voluntary proceeding under any bankruptcy
         or insolvency law; (ii) an involuntary proceeding is commenced against Borrower
         and not dismissed within sixty (60) days; (iii) Borrower makes a general
         assignment for the benefit of creditors; or (iv) appointment of a receiver
         or trustee over any material portion of Borrower's assets;

     (f) Change of Control: Any Change of Control of Borrower without Lender's prior
         written consent, not to be unreasonably withheld;

     (g) Material Adverse Change: Lender determines in its reasonable judgment that a
         Material Adverse Change has occurred in the business, financial condition, or
         prospects of the Borrower;

     (h) IP Assignment: Any purported assignment, sale, or transfer of material
         intellectual property outside the ordinary course of business.

3.2  Remedies: Upon an Event of Default, Lender may: (a) declare all outstanding principal,
accrued interest, and fees immediately due and payable; (b) exercise any right, power, or
remedy available at law or in equity; (c) foreclose on any security interest or lien granted
hereunder.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE IV — INDEMNIFICATION CLAUSE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4.1  General Indemnification: Borrower shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Lender
and its officers, directors, members, employees, advisors, successors, and assigns
(collectively, "Indemnified Parties") from and against any and all claims, damages, losses,
costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or relating to:
(a) any breach of this Agreement by Borrower; (b) any inaccuracy in any representation or
warranty; (c) the use or application of the Loan proceeds; (d) any claim by a third party
arising from Borrower's business operations; or (e) enforcement of this Agreement.

4.2  Procedure: In the event of any claim subject to indemnification, Lender shall promptly
notify Borrower in writing. Borrower shall assume control of the defence with counsel
reasonably acceptable to Lender. Lender shall cooperate in the defence and shall not settle
any claim for which it seeks indemnification without Borrower's prior written consent.

4.3  Survival: The indemnification obligations under this Article IV shall survive the
repayment of the Loan and termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE V — GOVERNING LAW CLAUSE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

5.1  Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with
the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict-of-law principles.
The parties irrevocably consent to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal
courts located in San Francisco County, California for any dispute arising hereunder.

5.2  Waiver of Jury Trial: Each party hereby irrevocably waives all right to trial by
jury in any action, proceeding, or counterclaim arising out of or related to this Agreement.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ARTICLE VI — GENERAL PROVISIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

6.1  Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties
with respect to the subject matter hereof.

6.2  Amendments: No amendment shall be binding unless executed in writing by both parties.

6.3  Severability: If any provision is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining
provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
