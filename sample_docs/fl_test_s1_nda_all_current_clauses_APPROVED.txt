NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 15 May 2026 between
Meridian Capital Partners LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Disclosing Party")
and Vantage Analytics Inc., a California corporation ("Receiving Party").

1. PURPOSE
The parties wish to explore a potential business collaboration involving proprietary
financial modelling methodologies and client portfolio strategies. This Agreement
governs all confidential information exchanged in connection with that evaluation.

2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by either party,
including but not limited to: trade secrets, financial models, client lists, pricing data,
software algorithms, and business strategies, whether disclosed orally, in writing, or
by any other means.

3. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY
The Receiving Party shall: (a) hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
using at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential
information, but in no event less than reasonable care; (b) not disclose Confidential
Information to any third party without prior written consent; (c) use Confidential
Information solely for the purpose of evaluating the potential collaboration.

4. FORCE MAJEURE CLAUSE
Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations
under this Agreement where such failure or delay results from any cause beyond that
party's reasonable control, including but not limited to: acts of God, flood, drought,
earthquake, or other natural disaster; epidemic or pandemic; terrorist attack, civil
war, civil commotion or riots; war, threat of or preparation for war; nuclear, chemical
or biological contamination; any law or action taken by a government or public
authority; collapse of buildings, fire, explosion or accident; any labour or trade
disputes, strikes, industrial action or lockouts; or interruption or failure of utility
service. The affected party shall give prompt written notice to the other party and
shall use commercially reasonable efforts to resume performance as soon as practicable.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL,
CONSEQUENTIAL, SPECIAL, EXEMPLARY OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS
AGREEMENT, EVEN IF SUCH PARTY HAS BEEN ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES.
Each party's total cumulative liability arising out of or related to this Agreement,
whether in contract, tort or under any other theory of liability, shall not exceed the
total fees paid or payable under any ancillary service agreement between the parties in
the six (6) months preceding the claim giving rise to such liability.

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION CLAUSE
Any dispute, controversy or claim arising out of or relating to this Agreement, or the
breach, termination or invalidity thereof, shall first be submitted to non-binding
mediation administered by JAMS within thirty (30) days of written notice. If mediation
fails to resolve the dispute within sixty (60) days of initiation, either party may
submit the dispute to binding arbitration administered by JAMS pursuant to its
Comprehensive Arbitration Rules and Procedures. The arbitration shall take place in
New York, New York, before a single arbitrator mutually agreed upon by the parties.
The award shall be final and binding and may be entered as a judgment in any court of
competent jurisdiction.

7. TERM AND TERMINATION
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of three (3) years from the date
first written above, unless earlier terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days
written notice. The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination for an
additional period of five (5) years.

8. GENERAL PROVISIONS
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to
the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations,
warranties and understandings. This Agreement may not be amended except by written
instrument signed by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first
written above.
