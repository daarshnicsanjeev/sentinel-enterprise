─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 1 — COVERAGE SCOPE AND LIMITS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1.1  Insuring Agreement: Subject to the terms, conditions, exclusions, and limitations of
this Policy, HPLL agrees to indemnify the Insured against Claims first made against the
Insured during the Policy Period for:

     (a) Wrongful Acts: Any actual or alleged negligent act, error, omission, misstatement,
         misleading statement, or breach of professional duty committed by the Insured or
         any person for whom the Insured is legally liable, in the performance of or failure
         to perform Professional Services;

     (b) Infringement of Intellectual Property: Unintentional infringement of copyright,
         trademark, or trade dress in the performance of Professional Services.

1.2  Limit of Liability:
     - Any One Claim:             GBP 5,000,000
     - Aggregate Annual Limit:    GBP 10,000,000
     - Defence Costs (within limit): Covered up to GBP 2,000,000 per claim

1.3  Retroactive Date: Coverage applies to Wrongful Acts occurring on or after 1 March 2021
(the Retroactive Date). Claims arising from acts prior to the Retroactive Date are excluded.

1.4  Territorial Scope: Worldwide, provided that claims are brought in a court of competent
jurisdiction in the United Kingdom, European Union member states, United States, Canada,
Australia, or New Zealand.

1.5  Professional Services Covered: Management consulting, financial advisory, M&A due
diligence, restructuring advice, ESG advisory, and regulatory compliance consulting, as
described in the Schedule of Professional Services attached hereto as Exhibit A.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 2 — EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS CLAUSE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2.1  This Policy does not cover Claims arising from:

     (a) Fraud and Dishonesty: Any dishonest, fraudulent, criminal, or intentionally
         wrongful act or omission by the Insured or any insured person, provided that
         coverage shall be maintained for innocent co-insureds;

     (b) Bodily Injury or Property Damage: Claims for bodily injury, sickness, disease,
         death, or loss of or damage to tangible property (other than data);

     (c) Prior Known Claims: Any Claim, circumstance, or potential claim that the Insured
         knew of or had reason to know of prior to the inception date of this Policy;

     (d) Insured vs. Insured: Claims brought by one insured person against another, or
         by any entity that is an affiliate, subsidiary, or parent of the Insured;

     (e) Contractual Liability: Liability assumed by the Insured under any contract or
         agreement beyond what would arise in absence of such contract, unless the Insured
         would have been liable in any event;

     (f) Regulatory Fines and Penalties: Fines, penalties, punitive damages, or
         exemplary damages imposed by any government, regulatory body, or court;

     (g) Insolvency or Financial Failure: Loss arising from the insolvency, bankruptcy,
         or financial failure of any client, investment, or third party;

     (h) War and Terrorism: Loss arising from war, invasion, civil war, terrorism,
         or nuclear, biological, chemical, or radiological contamination.

2.2  Deductible: The Insured shall bear the first GBP 25,000 of each Claim (inclusive of
defence costs) as a deductible before coverage applies.

SECTION 3 — CLAIMS PROCEDURE
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

3.1  Notification Obligation: The Insured must notify HPLL in writing as soon as
practicable but no later than sixty (60) days after first becoming aware of any Claim
or circumstance that may give rise to a Claim under this Policy. Late notification may
prejudice coverage at HPLL's discretion.

3.2  How to Notify: All claims notifications must be submitted to:
     Claims Department, Hartford Professional Lines Limited
     Fax: +44 20 7946 0222  |  Email: claims@hpll-insurance.com
     Reference: Policy No. PI-2026-UK-447821

3.3  Cooperation: The Insured shall:
     (a) Cooperate fully with HPLL and its appointed legal counsel;
     (b) Provide all information, documents, and assistance reasonably requested;
     (c) Not make any admission of liability, settlement offer, or ex-gratia payment
         without prior written consent of HPLL;
     (d) Preserve all documents, records, and electronic data relevant to any Claim.

3.4  Defence and Control of Claims: HPLL reserves the right to appoint legal counsel
and control the defence and settlement of any Claim covered under this Policy. HPLL
will consult with the Insured on strategy but retains final decision-making authority
on settlement offers within the Policy Limit.

3.5  Subrogation: Upon payment of a Claim, HPLL shall be subrogated to all rights of
the Insured against any third party responsible for the Claim to the extent of amounts
paid. The Insured shall cooperate fully with HPLL in any subrogation proceedings.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SECTION 4 — PREMIUM PAYMENT AND CANCELLATION TERMS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4.1  Annual Premium: The premium for this Policy is GBP 48,750 (exclusive of Insurance
Premium Tax at the applicable rate, currently 12%), payable as follows:
     - Instalment 1: GBP 24,375 due by 1 March 2026 (Policy inception)
     - Instalment 2: GBP 24,375 due by 1 September 2026

4.2  Late Payment: Premium instalments not received within fifteen (15) days of the due
date shall attract interest at 2% per month on the overdue balance.

4.3  Cancellation by Insured: The Insured may cancel this Policy by providing thirty (30)
days' prior written notice to HPLL. HPLL will refund the pro-rata unearned premium, less
a short-rate penalty of 10% of the annual premium.

4.4  Cancellation by HPLL: HPLL may cancel this Policy for:
     (a) Non-payment of premium, upon ten (10) days' written notice; or
     (b) Material misrepresentation or fraud, effective immediately upon written notice.

4.5  Run-Off Coverage: Upon cancellation or non-renewal, the Insured may purchase a
run-off extension extending the Claims-Made period for an additional three (3) years.
Run-off premium: 175% of the final annual premium rate.
