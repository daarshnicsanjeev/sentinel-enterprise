SENIOR SECURED CREDIT AGREEMENT

This Senior Secured Credit Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 20 May 2026
between Northbridge Commercial Finance Ltd., an English company ("Lender") and
Pellucid Manufacturing Group Inc., a Texas corporation ("Borrower").

RECITALS

The Lender has agreed to make available to the Borrower a revolving credit facility
of up to USD 25,000,000 (Twenty-Five Million United States Dollars) on the terms
and conditions set out in this Agreement.

1. DEFINITIONS

"Facility" means the revolving credit facility described in Clause 2.
"Drawdown Date" means any Business Day on which the Borrower makes a drawing.
"Interest Period" means each consecutive period of one (1) month, or such other
period as the parties may agree.
"Security Package" means all collateral granted to the Lender pursuant to the
Security Documents.

2. FACILITY AND DRAWDOWN
The Lender agrees to make available to the Borrower a revolving credit facility of
USD 25,000,000. Drawings shall be made in minimum amounts of USD 500,000. The
Borrower may request a drawing by delivering a duly completed Drawdown Notice to
the Lender no later than 10:00 a.m. (London time) three (3) Business Days prior to
the proposed Drawdown Date.

3. INTEREST AND FEES
Interest shall accrue on each drawing at a rate per annum equal to Term SOFR plus
a margin of 3.50% per annum, calculated on the basis of actual days elapsed over
a 360-day year. The Borrower shall pay a commitment fee of 1.00% per annum on
the undrawn and uncancelled amount of the Facility, payable quarterly in arrears.

4. GOVERNING LAW CLAUSE
This Agreement and any non-contractual obligations arising out of or in connection
with it shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State
of New York, without regard to its conflict of law provisions. Each party irrevocably
submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the federal and state courts located in
New York County, New York.

5. OBLIGOR ACCELERATION EVENTS
The following circumstances shall each constitute an "Obligor Acceleration Event"
(understood by the parties to be equivalent to what is commonly termed events of
default in standard credit documentation), the occurrence of any one of which shall
entitle the Lender, by written notice to the Borrower, to declare all outstanding
amounts immediately due and payable and to enforce the Security Package without
further formality:

(a) Non-Payment: the Borrower fails to pay any sum due under this Agreement on
    its due date, unless such failure is due to an administrative or technical
    error and is remedied within three (3) Business Days;

(b) Covenant Breach: the Borrower breaches any covenant, undertaking, condition
    or provision of this Agreement (other than a payment obligation) and, if
    capable of remedy, fails to remedy such breach within twenty (20) Business Days
    of the Lender's written notice;

(c) Insolvency: the Borrower becomes insolvent, is unable to pay its debts as
    they fall due, makes a general assignment for the benefit of its creditors,
    or a receiver, administrator or liquidator is appointed over all or any
    material part of its assets;

(d) Material Adverse Change: any event or circumstance occurs which, in the
    Lender's reasonable opinion, has or is reasonably likely to have a material
    adverse effect on the Borrower's ability to perform its obligations under
    this Agreement;

(e) Cross-Default: any financial indebtedness of the Borrower in excess of
    USD 1,000,000 becomes due and payable prior to its stated maturity, or any
    creditor of the Borrower becomes entitled to declare such indebtedness so due.

6. MUTUAL HOLD-HARMLESS AND COST-SHIFTING UNDERTAKING
Each party (the "Indemnifying Party") shall fully protect, hold harmless, defend,
and make whole the other party and its affiliates, directors, officers, employees,
agents and successors (each an "Indemnified Person") from and against any and all
claims, losses, damages, costs, charges, expenses, penalties, fines, liabilities and
judgments (collectively, "Losses") arising out of or in connection with: (a) any
breach by the Indemnifying Party of its obligations under this Agreement; (b) any
third-party claim attributable to the acts or omissions of the Indemnifying Party;
or (c) any inaccuracy in the representations and warranties of the Indemnifying Party.
This mutual hold-harmless obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

NOTE TO COMPLIANCE REVIEWER: The clause in Section 6 above serves as the
indemnification clause for this agreement. The heading "Mutual Hold-Harmless and
Cost-Shifting Undertaking" is used in accordance with Northbridge's in-house drafting
conventions but is substantively identical to a standard indemnification provision.

7. REPRESENTATIONS, WARRANTIES AND FACTUAL ACKNOWLEDGEMENTS
The Borrower represents, warrants and acknowledges as true on the date of this
Agreement and on each Drawdown Date:

(a) Corporate Standing: the Borrower is duly incorporated, validly existing and
    in good standing under the laws of its jurisdiction of incorporation;

(b) Authority: the Borrower has full corporate power and authority to execute,
    deliver and perform this Agreement; all necessary corporate action has been taken;

(c) No Conflict: the execution and performance of this Agreement does not violate
    any law, regulation, order, or the Borrower's constitutional documents, or any
    agreement to which the Borrower is a party;

(d) Financial Statements: the Borrower's most recent audited financial statements
    give a true and fair view of its financial condition and have been prepared in
    accordance with US GAAP consistently applied;

(e) No Litigation: there is no pending or, to the Borrower's knowledge, threatened
    litigation, arbitration or administrative proceeding that would reasonably be
    expected to have a material adverse effect on the Borrower.

8. SECURITY
As security for the Borrower's obligations under this Agreement, the Borrower shall,
on or before the first Drawdown Date, grant to the Lender: (a) a first-priority
perfected security interest in all accounts receivable and inventory; (b) a first
mortgage over the Borrower's manufacturing facility located at 4400 Industrial
Parkway, Houston, Texas 77001; and (c) a pledge over all shares of each material
subsidiary of the Borrower.

9. TERM AND AVAILABILITY
The Facility shall be available for drawing during the Availability Period, being the
period from the date of this Agreement until the date falling thirty-six (36) months
thereafter (the "Termination Date"). On the Termination Date all outstanding drawings
shall be repaid and the Facility shall be cancelled.

10. GENERAL
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties relating to the
Facility and supersedes all prior negotiations. Amendments require written consent
of both parties. If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions continue
in full force and effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first
written above.
