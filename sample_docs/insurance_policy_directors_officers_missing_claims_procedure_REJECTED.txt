DIRECTORS AND OFFICERS LIABILITY INSURANCE POLICY

Policy Number: DO-2026-018847
Insurer: Pinnacle Professional Lines Insurance
Named Insured: Stratosphere Ventures Inc. and its Subsidiaries
Policy Period: February 1, 2026 to January 31, 2027
Premium: USD 145,000 per annum

NOTE: This policy intentionally omits a claims procedure section to test compliance rejection.

DECLARATIONS

Pinnacle Professional Lines Insurance ("Insurer") agrees to provide Directors and Officers Liability Insurance to the Named Insured for the policy period stated above, subject to the terms, conditions, and limitations set forth herein.

1. COVERAGE SCOPE AND LIMITS

1.1 Side A Coverage — Non-Indemnifiable Director/Officer Claims
Limit: USD 10,000,000 per claim; USD 20,000,000 aggregate
This coverage protects individual directors and officers when the company is legally prohibited from or financially unable to indemnify them. No retention applies to Side A coverage.

1.2 Side B Coverage — Corporate Reimbursement
Limit: USD 20,000,000 per claim; USD 40,000,000 aggregate
Retention: USD 250,000 per claim
This coverage reimburses the Company when it indemnifies directors and officers for covered claims.

1.3 Side C Coverage — Entity Securities Claims
Limit: USD 15,000,000 per claim; USD 30,000,000 aggregate
Retention: USD 500,000 per claim
This coverage protects the entity itself for securities claims alleging violation of securities laws or regulations.

1.4 Crisis Management Coverage
Sublimit: USD 500,000
Covers public relations costs and crisis management expenses incurred following a covered event, including regulatory investigations, securities class actions, and shareholder derivative suits.

1.5 Investigation Costs Coverage
Sublimit: USD 2,000,000
Covers reasonable legal defense costs incurred in responding to formal regulatory investigations by the SEC, DOJ, FTC, or equivalent regulatory authorities, prior to the filing of formal charges.

2. EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS

2.1 The following losses are expressly excluded from coverage under this Policy:
(a) Bodily injury, property damage, personal injury, or advertising injury;
(b) Deliberate fraud, criminal conduct, or intentional wrongful acts, where established by final adjudication in an underlying proceeding;
(c) Claims by one Insured Person against another Insured Person (Insured vs. Insured exclusion), except for employment practices claims;
(d) Claims arising from the manufacture, distribution, or sale of products or services (covered under Product Liability policies);
(e) Prior acts known prior to the Retroactive Date of February 1, 2018;
(f) ERISA violations and pension fund mismanagement claims;
(g) Nuclear, biological, chemical, or radiological incident liability;
(h) Profit disgorgement or return of compensation where the Insured did not have a legal entitlement to such compensation, as determined by final adjudication;
(i) Pollution liability or environmental claims;
(j) Claims arising from any Insured serving as a director or officer of an entity other than the Named Insured, unless such service is at the direction of and in the interest of the Named Insured.

2.2 The Insurer's liability under this Policy shall not exceed the applicable limit of liability stated above, regardless of the number of claims, claimants, or insured persons involved.

3. PREMIUM PAYMENT AND CANCELLATION TERMS

3.1 Premium Payment Schedule
The annual premium of USD 145,000 is payable in full within 30 days of the Policy Inception Date of February 1, 2026. Payment options include wire transfer, ACH, or certified check payable to Pinnacle Professional Lines Insurance.

3.2 Installment Option
The Insured may request a semi-annual payment plan of USD 74,675 per instalment (reflecting a 3% instalment surcharge), subject to the Insurer's approval and execution of an Instalment Payment Agreement.

3.3 Non-Payment
Failure to pay the premium within 30 days of the due date shall result in a notice of cancellation with a 10-day cure period. Upon expiry of the cure period, coverage under this Policy shall be suspended until payment is received in full.

3.4 Cancellation by Named Insured
The Named Insured may cancel this Policy at any time by providing written notice to the Insurer. Refund of unearned premium shall be calculated on a short-rate basis (90% of the pro-rata unearned premium).

3.5 Cancellation by Insurer
The Insurer may cancel this Policy upon ninety (90) days' written notice to the Named Insured or upon ten (10) days' notice for non-payment of premium. Earned premium shall be calculated on a pro-rata basis, and any unearned premium shall be promptly refunded.

4. GENERAL CONDITIONS

4.1 Notice of Circumstances
If during the Policy Period the Insured becomes aware of circumstances which may reasonably give rise to a claim, the Insured may give written notice of such circumstances to the Insurer. Such notice shall be treated as a claim made during the Policy Period.

4.2 Cooperation
Insured Persons shall cooperate with the Insurer in the investigation and defense of any claim. The Insured shall not admit liability, make settlements, or incur defense costs in excess of USD 25,000 without the Insurer's prior written consent, except in an emergency.

4.3 Subrogation
Upon payment of any loss under this Policy, the Insurer shall be subrogated to all rights of recovery of the Insured against any party. The Insured agrees to assist the Insurer in pursuing such rights of recovery.

4.4 Entire Agreement
This Policy, together with the Application (which is incorporated by reference), constitutes the entire agreement between the Insurer and the Insured with respect to the subject matter hereof. Any modification must be in writing signed by an authorised representative of the Insurer.
