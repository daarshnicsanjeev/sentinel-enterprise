Borrower represents and warrants to Lender as follows:

Borrower has not omitted any material information that would reasonably affect Lender's decision to make this loan. Specifically: (a) Borrower's combined gross annual income is USD 218,000, as documented by tax returns and employer verification; (b) Borrower has no undisclosed liabilities, debts, or financial obligations that would materially affect their ability to repay this loan; (c) Borrower intends to occupy the Mortgaged Property as their primary residence within 60 days of closing.

1.2 Title and Property Condition
Borrower represents that, to their knowledge: (a) the Mortgaged Property is not subject to any encumbrances, liens, or claims other than those disclosed in the title insurance commitment; (b) Borrower has no knowledge of any structural defects, environmental hazards, or material conditions affecting the Mortgaged Property that have not been disclosed to Lender; (c) the Mortgaged Property is located in Flood Zone X (minimal flood hazard) as shown on FEMA Flood Insurance Rate Map effective date January 15, 2024.

1.3 No Litigation
There is no pending litigation, judgment, or claim that could affect Borrower's ability to repay this loan or that would result in a lien on the Mortgaged Property.

1.4 Occupancy Intent
Borrower intends to use the Mortgaged Property as their primary residence and shall not convert the property to a rental or investment property without prior written consent of the Lender.

2. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Each of the following shall constitute an "Event of Default":

2.1 Payment Default
Borrower fails to make any monthly payment within fifteen (15) days of the due date. A late charge of 5% of the overdue monthly payment shall be assessed for each payment received more than 15 days after the due date.

2.2 Abandonment
Borrower abandons or vacates the Mortgaged Property without Lender's written consent for a period exceeding sixty (60) consecutive days.

2.3 Transfer Without Consent (Due-on-Sale)
Borrower sells, transfers, or conveys any interest in the Mortgaged Property without Lender's prior written consent. Lender may declare the entire outstanding balance immediately due and payable upon any unauthorized transfer.

2.4 Breach of Representations
Any material misrepresentation made in the mortgage application or this Agreement is discovered and Lender determines the loan would not have been made had the accurate information been known.

2.5 Hazard Insurance Lapse
Borrower fails to maintain hazard insurance, flood insurance (if required), and title insurance on the Mortgaged Property with Lender named as loss payee/additional insured.

2.6 Tax Default
Borrower fails to pay any real property taxes or assessments when due, which creates a lien senior to Lender's mortgage, and such failure continues for more than sixty (60) days.

2.7 Bankruptcy or Insolvency
Borrower files for voluntary bankruptcy protection, or an involuntary bankruptcy petition is filed against Borrower and not dismissed within sixty (60) days.

2.8 Property Deterioration
Borrower allows the Mortgaged Property to deteriorate to a degree that materially impairs Lender's security interest, as determined by a licensed appraiser engaged at Lender's discretion.

3. INDEMNIFICATION

3.1 Environmental Indemnification
Borrower shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Lender and its successors, assigns, officers, and employees from and against any and all claims, costs, losses, and liabilities arising from any environmental contamination, hazardous materials, or violation of environmental law at or emanating from the Mortgaged Property during Borrower's ownership.
