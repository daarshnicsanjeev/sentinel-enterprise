SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2025, between Meridian Financial Partners Ltd. ("Service Provider") and Crestwood Capital Group Inc. ("Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Service Provider agrees to deliver financial advisory and document management services as outlined in Schedule A. The engagement shall commence on February 1, 2025, and continue for a period of twelve (12) months unless otherwise terminated.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client shall remit payment within thirty (30) days of receipt of invoice. Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month. All fees are denominated in USD and are non-refundable.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary information exchanged under this Agreement. This obligation survives termination of the Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

4. TERM AND TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice. Upon termination, all outstanding fees become immediately due and payable.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All deliverables produced by Service Provider under this Agreement shall remain the exclusive property of Client upon full payment of fees.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
