SYNDICATED TERM LOAN FACILITY AGREEMENT

FACILITY AMOUNT: USD 500,000,000

This Syndicated Term Loan Facility Agreement ("Agreement") dated February 28, 2026, is entered into among:

BORROWER: Titanfall Infrastructure Holdings Ltd., a Cayman Islands exempted company ("Borrower")
ARRANGER: JPMorgan Chase Bank N.A. ("Arranger")
AGENT: JPMorgan Chase Bank N.A., as Administrative Agent ("Agent")
LENDERS: The financial institutions listed on Schedule A hereto (collectively, "Lenders")

WHEREAS, the Borrower has requested a senior secured term loan facility of up to USD 500,000,000 to finance the acquisition of certain infrastructure assets in South and Southeast Asia.

1. THE FACILITY
1.1 Commitment. Subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein, each Lender severally agrees to make a Term Loan to the Borrower on the Closing Date in an amount not to exceed such Lender's Commitment as set forth on Schedule A.
1.2 Facility Amount. The aggregate principal amount of the Term Loan Facility shall not exceed FIVE HUNDRED MILLION UNITED STATES DOLLARS (USD 500,000,000).
1.3 Purpose. The proceeds of the Term Loan shall be used solely to finance the acquisition of Indus Tower Assets Portfolio and for general corporate purposes of the Borrower.

2. INTEREST
2.1 Rate. The Term Loan shall bear interest at SOFR plus a margin of 285 basis points per annum, adjusted quarterly.
2.2 Default Interest. Upon the occurrence of an Event of Default, the outstanding principal shall bear interest at the applicable rate plus 200 basis points per annum.

3. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES
The Borrower represents and warrants to the Agent and each Lender, on the date hereof and on the Closing Date:
(a) Organization: The Borrower is duly organized, validly existing and in good standing under the laws of the Cayman Islands.
(b) Authorization: The execution, delivery and performance of this Agreement have been duly authorized by all necessary corporate action.
(c) No Conflict: The execution and performance of this Agreement does not violate any provision of law, any order of any court, or the terms of any material agreement to which the Borrower is a party.
(d) Financial Statements: The financial statements of the Borrower delivered to the Agent fairly present in all material respects the financial condition of the Borrower as of the dates thereof.
(e) No Material Adverse Change: Since December 31, 2025, there has been no material adverse change in the business, assets, liabilities, operations, condition (financial or otherwise) or prospects of the Borrower.
(f) Litigation: There are no actions, suits or proceedings pending or, to the Borrower's knowledge, threatened against or affecting the Borrower which could reasonably be expected to have a Material Adverse Effect.

4. EVENTS OF DEFAULT
Each of the following events shall constitute an "Event of Default":
(a) Payment Default: The Borrower fails to pay any principal when due, or fails to pay any interest or fees within three (3) Business Days of when due.
(b) Covenant Breach: The Borrower fails to observe or perform any material covenant or agreement contained in this Agreement (other than those described in clause (a) above) and such failure continues unremedied for thirty (30) days after written notice.
(c) Misrepresentation: Any representation or warranty made herein proves to have been false in any material respect when made.
(d) Cross Default: Any Event of Default or similar event under any other Debt Instrument of the Borrower in excess of USD 25,000,000 in aggregate.
(e) Insolvency: The Borrower (i) becomes insolvent; (ii) admits in writing its inability to pay debts generally; (iii) makes a general assignment for the benefit of creditors; or (iv) has an involuntary proceeding commenced against it under any Debtor Relief Law.
(f) Change of Control: Any Change of Control occurs without the prior written consent of Lenders holding at least 66.67% of outstanding Commitments.

5. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement and any non-contractual obligations arising out of or in connection with it shall be governed by and construed in accordance with English law. The parties irrevocably agree that the courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.

6. INDEMNIFICATION
The Borrower shall indemnify and hold harmless the Agent, the Arranger, and each Lender and their respective officers, directors, employees, agents, and advisors (each, an "Indemnified Party") from and against any and all losses, claims, damages, liabilities, and related expenses (including the fees, charges, and disbursements of counsel) incurred by or asserted against any Indemnified Party arising out of, in connection with, or as a result of (i) the execution or delivery of this Agreement or any other Loan Document, (ii) the performance by the parties of their respective obligations hereunder, or (iii) any actual or prospective claim, litigation, investigation or proceeding relating to any of the foregoing, regardless of whether any Indemnified Party is a party thereto.
